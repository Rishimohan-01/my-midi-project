MiDi

Technopreneurs India

Maharashtra Industries Development Institute more commonly known as ‘MiDi’, is emerging as one of this leading institute for Technology Research and Development, Industrial & Technical Consultancy and Bank Support Services for Micro Small & Medium Entrepreneurs (MSMEs) in all over India .

MiDi was born out of the need to provide professional and technology training on entrepreneurship development to a wide range of potential and existing entrepreneurs at national level. MiDi essentially serves as a guide for entrepreneurs who have the skill and talent but lack of training, financial, Innovative Technology and other elementary resources to set up their own business enterprises. It also makes young entrepreneurs aware of the various Government schemes, incentives and subsidies available to them. To find out the gap of technology development in the market for MSMEs, MiDi act as a Innovative technology provider to Indian MSMEs to become competitive at global markets. Our Research & Scientist team will assess the technology to fit suitable to concerned industry segment to achieve the desired Goal.

Our Vision & Mission

MiDi will provide new ideas and innovative technologies to fulfil company’s business needs and to provide services that can increase productivity and profits in global environment.

Provide modern and innovative technologies to the market and to play a vital role in global competitive market.

Transfer innovative technologies, products and processes to the industries and contribute in the technology development of the country.

Creation of sustainable livelihoods and employment opportunities.

Promoting & eco friendly technology & equitable economic Growth.

Improving productivity and reduce Drudgery.

Promoting efficient and effective use of local and natural resources.
