OUR VISION & MISSION

Our Mission

Maharashtra Industries Development Institute is Committed to providing hand holding support to Micro,Small and Medium Scale Enterprises(MSMEs) and strengthens their hands by providing quality training,consultancy in the related fields, and facilitate in procuring finance.We aim to become premier provider and facilitators of quality services to MSMEs/prospective entrepreneurs in line with the Government to serve MSME Sector.

Our Vision

To establish ourselves as a major player in our area of operations through dedication,passion and professionalism. To Grow in to an Organization that has excellent facilities and talents to help meet the challenges of establishing, growing, sustaining and expanding of MSMEs. To help and encourage prospective entrepreneurs to take up self employment and thereby generating employment and creating wealth for the nation To forge our resources to help our customers forge ahead.

Our Activities

MiDi is a multi-faceted institute with major presence throughout Maharashtra and outside, in areas like:

  • Industrial & Technical Consultancy
  • Loan Facilitation & Syndication Services
  • MSME Subsidy/Incentives
  • Facilitating Bank Support Services, Feasibility-TEV Study
  • Skill Development & Vocational Training
  • Entrepreneurship Development Programmes
  • Facilitating MSME growth, SME summits
  • Project Management and Strategic Planning
  • Market Development
  • Special Handholding Supports to MSMEs
  • Technology Development
  • Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) Support
  • Franchising of Vocational Training Centre (ATC)

In Short, MiDi provides hand holding support to prospective, budding and existing entrepreneurs through training,consultancy and by facilitating bank finance
